About CSE

Computer Science and Engineering(CSE) is a highly interdisciplinary area spanning a broad range of science and engineering topics. The department of CSE at G.V.P College of Engineering for Women provides a good knowledge environment complemented by excellence in teaching.

IT

The Department of Information Technology(IT) at GVPW aims to equip the students with fundamental concepts and techniques of computing and applications relevant to emerging technological advancements. The curriculum designed by JNTUK is followed by the department and focuses on providing fundamentals of theory followed by practical projects and industrial training.

03

EEE

The department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering(EEE)was established in the year 2011. The main objective of the department is to produce high quality Electrical Engineering graduates. The department flourishes in its enthusiastic and energetic environment comprising of the major fields of Electrical, Electronics and Elements of Computer Science. The department has state of the art laboratories that are equipped with instruments and equipment for teaching, learning and research.
